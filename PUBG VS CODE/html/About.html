About Tiarastella Gaming site

Welcome to Tiarastella Gaming — a vibrant digital space dedicated to gamers, fans, and esports enthusiasts who wish to follow the journey of a passionate mobile gamer.

Founded in April 2025, this platform is a tribute to years of dedication and competitive gameplay in the mobile esports scene, particularly focusing on PUBG Mobile and Mobile Legends. It features a collection of strategic plays, ranked achievements, tournament highlights, and team contributions that define the gaming identity of the player behind it all.

At the heart of Tiarastella Gaming is Ms. Anis Faizah, the founder and sole owner of the in-game characters. With experience dating back to 2015, Anis has grown from a casual player into a formidable force within the mobile gaming community. Her journey is one of persistence, skill-building, and a strong passion for the gaming world.

Here, you’ll get to:

Explore Achievements : Check out milestone victories, tournament highlights, MVP titles, and rank progressions.

Track Gaming Stats : Dive into interactive graphs and detailed analytics showing kill or death ratios, win rates, damage per match, and more.

View the Gallery : Scroll through a curated collection of screenshots, gameplay clips, and memorable in-game moments.

Whether you're a fellow gamer or just curious, Tiarastella Gaming is the perfect place to witness real gaming experiences backed by skill, teamwork, and dedication.
